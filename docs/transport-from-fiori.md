# Transporting Changes done in Fiori to another System (SAP GUI)

> ⚠️ This runbook is still being maintained.

1. Open **Fiori Launchpad**.
2. Go to the search bar and type: **Register Extensions for Transport**
3. From the search bar in the program, look for your object.
4. If your object is not tranportable, you have to **reassign the package**.
5. After you click **Reassign to Package**, choose your desired package.
